Instructions


You will be presented with 40 statements and a scalar ranging from agree to disagree. For each one, click the button with your opinion on it.


Remember: Every question can be answered from a range. The questions are not meant to be answered as ONLY purely right or purely wrong, unless that is, of course, your opinion on the matter.


Press the 'Back' button to go back to the previous question answered.


The 'Nullify' button should ONLY to be used in emergency situations, for example, if a question just does not resonate with you at all, then press the button to essentially NULLIFY your answer, so as to not put you as a centrist for a question you cannot answer.


If you want to help with the construction of the test, remember to record your answers for the questions.
